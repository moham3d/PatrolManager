# User Guide

This guide provides step-by-step instructions for the daily operations of PatrolShield for all user roles.

### Getting Started
1.  **Log In**: Access the web portal at your designated URL.
2.  **Dashboard**: You will land on the Site Dashboard, showing an overview of your assigned locations.

### Setting Up a New Site
1.  Navigate to **Sites** in the main menu.
2.  Click **Create New Site**.
3.  Enter site details: Name, Address, Description.
4.  **Configure Settings**:
    -   *Geofence Radius*: How close a guard must be to clock in (e.g., 100 meters).
    -   *Security Level*: Sets default alert thresholds.
5.  Click **Save**.

### Creating Zones and Checkpoints
1.  Open your Site details.
2.  Go to the **Zones** tab and click **Add Zone**.
    -   Define the zone type (e.g., "Parking", "Entrance").
3.  Inside a Zone, click **Add Checkpoint**.
    -   Select the verification method (QR Code or NFC).
    -   The system will generate a unique QR code for you to print and place at the physical location.

### Assigning Personnel
1.  Go to the **Users** tab within a Site.
2.  Click **Assign User**.
3.  Search for an existing user and select their role (Guard or Supervisor) for this specific site.
4.  They will now see this site in their mobile app.

### Monitoring Active Patrols
1.  Log in to the Web Portal or Mobile App using your Supervisor credentials.
2.  Navigate to **Patrols**.
3.  Select **Live View**.
4.  You will see a list of in-progress patrols. Clicking one will show the guard's progress through the checkpoints on a map.

### Handling Incidents
1.  When an incident is reported, you will receive a notification in the **Alerts** or **Incidents** panel.
2.  Click the alert to view details (photos, location, notes).
3.  **Take Action**:
    -   *Acknowledge*: Let the system know you are aware.
    -   *Escalate*: Forward to higher management if critical.
    -   *Resolve*: Mark as closed once the issue is handled.

## üõ°Ô∏è For Guards

### Starting Your Shift
1.  Open the PatrolShield App on your mobile device.
2.  Log in with your username and password.
3.  Tap **Start Shift**.
    -   *Note*: You must be physically present at the site (inside the Geofence) to clock in.

### Performing a Patrol
1.  Go to the **Patrols** tab.
2.  Select an assigned route from the list.
3.  Tap **Start Patrol**.
4.  Follow the list of checkpoints.
5.  **Scanning**:
    -   Tap **Scan** next to the current checkpoint.
    -   Point your camera at the QR code.
    -   The app will verify the scan and your GPS location.
6.  Once all checkpoints are visited, tap **Complete Patrol**.

### Reporting an Incident
1.  Tap the **Incident** icon at any time.
2.  Select the type of incident (e.g., Vandalism, Theft).
3.  Take a photo of the evidence.
4.  Add a brief description.
5.  Tap **Submit**.

### üö® Emergency SOS
**In case of immediate danger:**
1.  Tap the red **SOS** button on the bottom of the screen.
2.  Confirm the alert if prompted (unless "Silent Mode" is active).
3.  This sends an immediate high-priority alarm with your live location to all supervisors.
