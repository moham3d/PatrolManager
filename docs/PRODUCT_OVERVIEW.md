# Product Overview

PatrolShield is a modern security management platform that redefines how security companies manage their operations. It moves away from disjointed manual processes to a unified, site-centric digital workflow.

## Core Philosophy: Site-Centric Operations

Unlike legacy systems that treat guards or incidents as isolated data points, PatrolShield anchors everything to the **Site**.

1.  **Site Creation**: A physical location is defined with specific boundaries (Geofencing) and requirements.
2.  **Zone Definition**: The site is broken down into specific zones (e.g., "North Parking", "Lobby", "Server Room").
3.  **Checkpoint Deployment**: Physical or digital checkpoints (QR Codes, NFC tags) are placed within zones.
4.  **Route Design**: Patrol routes are created by connecting checkpoints in a logical sequence.
5.  **Assignment**: Users (Guards, Supervisors) are assigned to the Site, granting them access to relevant data and tasks.

This hierarchy ensures that every action‚Äîwhether it's a patrol scan, an incident report, or a shift clock-in‚Äîis contextually linked to the correct location.
